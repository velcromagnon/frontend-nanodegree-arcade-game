Frogger Help

The objective of this game is to move your player up the screen to reach the water without being struck by one of the enemies (bugs). You gain an increasing score each level but your enemies become more difficult as well. You may unlock various upgrades, and gain points and health from powerups as well.

C Swap character (unlocked at level 5)

H or ? Show help
